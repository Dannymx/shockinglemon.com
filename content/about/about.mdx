## How to contribute

This website is only possible with the support of the fans, if you would like to contribute, we are currently looking for help in the following areas:

- Transcription and Translation of lyrics (found in the Music section).
- Finding any rare photos or videos.
- Finding the discs Marshmallow Bomb and Pastel Room, and any other lost media.

If you're also good with coding, you can submit a Pull Request to our public [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Dannymx/shockinglemon.com).

All contributions will be properly credited on this website.
