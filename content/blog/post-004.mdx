For more than 20 years fans of Shocking Lemon always wanted to see the video of the song Under Star,
it was believed to be long lost, only a small portion of the video was found before through the Wayback Machine.

But now fans can rejoice, as VAP just reuploaded several videos of their artists under the label, including the long lost video of Under Star.

Under Star and Inner Light were uploaded now in HD quality (1080p) to their youtube channel.

You can watch the videos below.
